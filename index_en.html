Who We Are?

At Meditech, we are committed to transforming the way dental professionals manage their clinics by providing advanced and efficient tools that optimize daily operations and enhance the patient experience.

Mission

Simplify and optimize dental clinic management.

Goal

Become the leading dental clinic management platform in Lima.

Values

Quality and Excellence, Constant Innovation, and Social Responsibility to contribute positively.

What Do We Offer?

Our software is designed to simplify daily administration, allowing dental professionals to focus on what matters most: patient care.

Productivity

Optimizes dental clinic productivity by automating routine tasks and streamlining administrative processes.

Control and Management

Efficiently manages everything from appointment scheduling to clinical record handling and billing.

Technical Support

Includes specialized technical support, available to resolve any issues quickly.

Staff Management

Facilitates the organization and management of clinic staff, allowing tracking of schedules, tasks, and performance.
